Incident Report Pump P-101 Seal Failure
Asset: P-101 Centrifugal Process Pump
Date: 2026-06-02

Incident: repeated seal leakage observed after two high vibration alarms.

Evidence: vibration crossed 8.2 mm/s before seal failure. Bearing replacement was delayed due to shutdown availability.

Inventory note: mechanical seal stockout caused temporary operation with degraded seal condition.

Likely failure mode: seal leakage linked to bearing wear, shaft misalignment, and inadequate lubrication.

Immediate action: stop pump, isolate energy, replace mechanical seal, inspect bearing kit, and verify alignment.
