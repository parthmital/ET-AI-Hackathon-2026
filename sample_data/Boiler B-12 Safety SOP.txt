Boiler B-12 Safety SOP
Asset: B-12 Package Boiler
Location: Boiler House

Procedure: Before servicing Boiler B-12 apply LOTO, verify zero stored pressure, and complete Permit To Work.

PPE requirement: flame-resistant clothing, face shield, heat-resistant gloves, safety shoes, and portable gas detector.

Confined-space permit is required when vessel entry or internal drum inspection is applicable.

Safety hazards: hot surface, steam release, confined space, and flammable gas.

Regulatory reference: Factory Safety Standard and ISO 45001.
