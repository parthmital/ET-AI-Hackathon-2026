Pump P-101 OEM Manual
Asset: P-101 Centrifugal Process Pump
Location: Unit 1 Process Area

The pump shall not operate continuously above vibration threshold of 7 mm/s. High vibration indicates bearing wear, shaft misalignment, or inadequate lubrication.

Recommended maintenance activity: bearing replacement after abnormal vibration trend and seal replacement after leakage is detected.

Mechanical seal and bearing kit must be available before planned shutdown.

Work order WO-1201 recorded vibration warning on 2026-04-11.

Safety hazard: rotating equipment and stored pressure. PPE requirement: safety shoes and face shield during seal replacement.
